Official Rule Book

Team Formation

  • Minimum 3 and maximum 5 participants per team, including the team leader.
  • Teams can include members from any year or department.

Themes:

Event Plan

Day 1 (21 May):

  • All team members must be present at the club desk at 1:30 A.M. at Atal Sabha Ghar.
  • Teams will develop their projects during the event day.
  • Club coordinators will be available for assistance.

Day 2:

  • Teams will prepare a PPT of their project.
  • All team members must be available for project presentations.
  • Each team will present their PPTs, and points will be given based on domain, tech-stack, PPT, teamwork, and involvement.

Security & Conduct

  • All participants will be issued an event-specific ID card and a band by the organizing team. These must be worn and visible at all times during the event.
  • Only participants wearing the official event ID card and band, and authorized personnel, are allowed in the event area.
  • Use of unauthorized electronic devices (e.g., hacking tools, external storage) is strictly prohibited.
  • Teams are responsible for backing up their work. The organizers are not liable for data loss.
  • Any form of misconduct, cheating, or disruptive behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
  • In case of emergency, follow the instructions of the event coordinators and security staff.

Event Logistics

  • All participants are free to leave at 5:00 PM on Day 1. Teams can return the next day to continue and complete their work.
  • Keep your belongings safe. The organizers are not responsible for any loss or theft.
